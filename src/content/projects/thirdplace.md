## Context

[Stopped production for a while.]

My friends and I wanted to create a social media app, focusing on the concept of the
"third place," which is anywhere that isn't home (the 1st place) and work (the 2nd place).
Whether that be a cafe, library, or rock-climbing gym, we all have our third place. 
We're not trying to compete with social media apps like Instagram directly, we're trying
to provide an unique edge.

## The Product

The product is currently being built through react native. The idea is to have three tabs: 
map, home, and profile. The map tab would have a fog-of-war feature, where it hides places in the world that the user has not been to yet. The home page would have feed updates. The profile page is the most interesting. It would have the user's profile as a social media app would, but have tags assigned by AI based on what kind of person the user is. For example, a person who hits the gym more than 3 times a week consistenly would have a "Gym Rat" tag that allows them to connect with others with a similar tag.
